Description

Option Calculator integrates modern design with advanced risk calculation models.

Our tool offers three distinct models:

  • OI: A basic model using option price, delta, ATR, days until expiration, and IV—adjusted by trade action and option type—to compute a risk metric and derive stop loss and take profit levels.
  • OI: An advanced model incorporating additional parameters such as theta, implied volatility, open interest, market sentiment, stock price, and contracts to compute a composite sensitivity score and detailed risk outputs.
  • OI+: A customizable model with a two‑column layout. The left column includes inputs with weight scales (IV, Delta, Theta, Open Interest), while the right column contains Option Price, Contracts, Days Till Expiration, and Sentiment (Bullish, Bearish, Consolidate). Additional sliders for max risk and reward multiplier determine the final output.
